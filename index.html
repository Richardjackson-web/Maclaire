Places Around Macaire Hotel

Standard amenities are provided for guests at the Macaire Hotel and Resorts include: a conference hall with facilities, an outdoor pool, bar/lounge with a variety of beverages on offer, event hall, outdoor pool bar with accompanying sit out.

10-Minutes Drive

Ikot Ekpene Plaza - The plaza consists of a long tree lined promenade with two outdoor dining areas either side of an exciting central fountain and is a venue for outdoor entertainment along with a large outdoor viewing screen to show soccer games and films. A children’s play area adds to the leisure provision for all of the town’s younger age groups. Ancillary facilities include a maintenance building, car park, public toilets and an access footbridge. It is a wonderful sight to behold and its 10 Minutes drive from Macaire Hotel.

11-Minutes Drive

Ikot Ekpene Township Stadium - The 5,000 capacity township stadium in Ikot Ekpene District is located in the heart of the city with state of the art facilities and is a great for recreational and competitive sports as well.
